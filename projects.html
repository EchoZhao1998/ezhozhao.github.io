CES Merchandise Design & Operations

Role: Design Lead & Operations · Scope: Design, Procurement, Logistics

Led the design and delivery of CES creative merchandise, including apparel, canvas bags, stationery, and accessories.

Beyond visual design, I oversaw supplier selection, cost comparison, quantity planning, and budget calculation. All items were manufactured in mainland China and shipped to the United States, requiring careful logistics planning to ensure on-time delivery to Harvard University in Boston within budget constraints.
